Frequently Asked Questions

Is this paid sponsorship or affiliate program?

No. This is a manual founder collaboration with no commission, referral fee, affiliate payout, or revenue share.

Do I automatically receive one free month after signing?

No. Complimentary access begins only after founder approval.

Why does signup show $79/month and seven-day access?

That is the public plan display. Approved founding creators may receive adjusted terms after manual review.

How do I unlock Month 2?

Month 2 is conditional on completing the agreed first-month content and feedback requirements and receiving approval.

Must the review be positive?

No. MARCOR only expects a truthful, fair, firsthand account.

What does the lifetime price lock mean?

If you remain continuously subscribed and in good standing, the founding partner rate is intended to stay locked at $49/month.

What happens if I cancel or fail payment?

The rate lock and founder partner status may be lost if continuity is broken or payment fails.

Are add-ons included forever?

No. Add-ons, usage overages, taxes, premium services, and future products are excluded unless specifically stated otherwise.

When do weekly one-on-one sessions start?

They may become available after the complimentary months for active paid partners, subject to scheduling and availability.

Is weekly support done-for-you implementation?

No. It is consultation and guidance only, not unlimited done-for-you implementation or emergency support.

Can MARCOR reuse my content?

Only if you give optional permission. Otherwise, you keep ownership of your original content.

Will I receive a signed PDF copy?

Yes. A signed A4 PDF is generated after successful submission and can be downloaded again from the success panel.

Who gives final approval?

Final approval is made manually by founder Dijae Dadula.
